What is Loan EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay your loan. It includes both principal and interest.

EMI Formula Loan

The standard EMI calculation formula is:

EMI = [P × r × (1 + r)n] / [(1 + r)n – 1]

Where:

  • P = Loan amount
  • r = Monthly interest rate (annual rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100)
  • n = Loan tenure (in months)
